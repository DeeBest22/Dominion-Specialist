Our Story

From its inception on September 1, 2011, Dominion Specialist had clearly defined its brand — Healthcare. Hospitality. Humaneness. We have endeavoured over the last one-and-half decades to adhere to these founding principles in order to deliver quality healthcare to our esteemed patients.

Our story began with the passion to ensure that trained and competent medical, nursing and paramedical staff provided services in an environment that is hospitable — comfortable, compassionate and without discrimination, yet affordable and easily accessible despite being in a resource-limited nation.

The testimonials from our clients continually confirm that we are headed in the right direction as to the delivery on our mandate. As we prepare to mark our 14th anniversary, we are strategically positioning the facility to become the most referenced health institution in the State within the next decade, providing comprehensive and patient-tailored care to the populace.

Meet Our Team

Chief Medical Director

Prof. Awoleke, Jacob Olumuyiwa

MBBS (Lagos), MSc (Liverpool John Moores), FWACS, FMCOG

Professor Awoleke is the Chief Medical Director, and the Pioneer Coordinator, Health Services and Training of the Centre. His extensive clinical experience in both private and public health institutions over the last three decades has moulded the outpatient services in Dominion Specialist into a responsive, patient-centered unit in the facility.

Since the hospital's creation in 2011, he has attended to hundreds of walk-in and emergency patients, spearheading their management and optimizing the standards-of-care protocol. He also provides clinical services to the Hospital via specialist prenatal clinics, gynaecological clinic sessions, and conduct of various obstetric and gynaecological surgeries.

He is also Chair, Feto-Maternal Medicine Unit of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology, College of Medicine, Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti, Nigeria, with research interests revolving around feto-maternal medicine and adolescent gynaecology/reproductive health issues.

Dr. Ige, Joshua Taye

MBBS, FWACS

General and Paediatric Surgeon

Dr. Ige is an accomplished General and Paediatric Surgeon with over three decades of post-specialist experience in his field. He has successfully conducted major surgical procedures — mastectomy, thyroidectomy, laparotomy for tumour excision etc, and other emergency interventions.

Nurse Akinyemi Toyin

RN, BNSc

Lead Nurse

As Lead Nurse, Toyin has served in various units of the hospital — outpatient, emergency department, operating room and prenatal clinics. She is a compassionate nurse who has competently and conscientiously cared for patients from all walks of life.
